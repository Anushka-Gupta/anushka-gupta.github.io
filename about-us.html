About Us

This website was created by two puzzled students who wanted to understand how to optimise their study period by increasing focus and memory with the least effort required. Faced with the challenge of learning something from one ear and letting it escape from the other, we decided a more personal and biological approach may do the trick! Using our senses, not only have we increased how much we study, but our overall confident in the studying process. By feeling confident on how we study, we lost the mentality of worrying about forgetting the course we learnt the previous day and basked in the happiness when we remember something our classmate didn't. And so, we wanted to share this confidence with the rest of the world!
