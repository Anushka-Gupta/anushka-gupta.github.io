Which type of learner are you?

something about learning. There are three types:

  1. kinesthetic
  2. viual
  3. audatory

quiz

Kinesthetic

kin is all about movement. You might learn in ways such as:

  • writing
  • dancing

Visual

vis is all about looking at stuff. You might learn in ways such as:

  • making posters
  • watching a video or documentary

Auditory

aud is all about listening. You might learn in ways such as:

  • listening to podcasts
  • recording yourslef and listening back. This is especially useful for learning new languages
