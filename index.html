Which type of learner are you?

something about learning. There are three types:

  1. kinesthetic
  2. viual
  3. audatory

quiz

Depending on your result, you take information in best by seeing, hearing or doing, dictating whether you are a visual, auditory or kinasthetic learner. If you are a visual learner, you find using pictures and diagrams the easiest to remember. If you an auditory learner, you understand information best by listening and speaking. If you are a Kinasthetic learner, then you learn best by doing. As general advice, you should use your predominant sense most but many prefer to use a mixture of the other senses too.
